Refund Policy

All sales are generally final due to the nature of the products.

Eligible Refund Cases

1. Full Refund: If the order is not shipped within 4 days of payment.

2. Partial Refund (excluding shipping charges and discounts): If the product delivered is significantly damaged and unusable.

3. Partial Refund (excluding shipping charges): If a completely incorrect item is delivered.

Non-Refundable Cases

No refunds will be provided for:

  • Minor wear, expected usage marks (2–5%)
  • Subjective dissatisfaction
  • Incorrect address or contact details provided by the customer
  • Failure of the customer to receive the delivery

Refund Request

Customers must contact within 48 hours of delivery with proper proof (photos/videos) to request any refund.
